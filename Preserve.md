**Preserve these — they ARE the game:**

1. **Class fantasy** — DW, DK, Elf, MG/SM, etc. Their skills, weapons, and role identity.
2. **Grindy PvE loop** — mob farming, repetitive leveling, no shortcuts. That's the addiction hook.
3. **Item system** — sockets, excellent/ancient items, +options, luck/skill bonuses, jewel-of-bless/soul crafting.
4. **Tetris inventory** — shaped items in a grid. Don't touch it.
5. **Loot on ground** — items drop and sit there, you walk over to pick up. Keep the visual moment of a rare item hitting the floor.
6. **Wings** — the wing progression system (1st/2nd/3rd wings) is peak MU dopamine. Don't cut or rush it.
7. **PK system** — red/yellow name, murder penalty. Core to server culture and tension.
8. **Castle Siege / guild wars** — the endgame social/PvP structure.
9. **Zen economy + item trading** — player-driven market feel.
10. **Art style / world tone** — dark medieval fantasy, Lorencia/Noria/Devias atmosphere. Don't reskin it into something generic.
11. **Sound design** — iconic hit sounds, level-up jingle, background music. People are nostalgic for the *audio* as much as visuals.

**Rule of thumb:** if it's about *identity, progression, or economy* — keep it. If it's about *how you move/aim/feel input* — modernize freely.
